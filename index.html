The Details

Alaina and Mike request the pleasure of your company as we join in marriage in the presence of family and friends.

Where

Brindabella Hills Winery

156 Woodgrove Cl, Hall, ACT 2618, AUSTRALIA

Located 25km (15 minutes) north of Canberra, Brindabella Hills Winery looks onto the Murrumbidgee river and Brindabella mountain ranges.

Guests will need to organise their own transportation to and from the venue via an Uber, taxi or car pool.

See it on a Map

When

12-14 March 2021

The wedding festivities will take place on the 13th of March for the ceremony and reception, with a brunch on the 14th of March.

Accommodation

The wedding venue does not have any accommodation onsite. For those travelling who need a place to stay, we suggest booking accommodation in Gungahlin at the Abode Hotel, or in Gold Creek at the Gold Creek Tourist Resort.

Alternatively, staying somewhere along the light rail route on Northborne avenue may help to facilitate travelling around Canberra.

Events

We'll start with a ceremony, share a meal together then move on to the reception. Dress code will be cocktail.

Wedding Ceremony

Sat, 13th March, 5.00 PM - 6.00 PM

Brindabella Hills Winery

We will start with a private ceremony at 5pm. Guests should aim to arrive at 4.30pm. Parking spots at the venue are limited.

Wedding Dinner

Sat, 13th March, 6.00 PM

Brindabella Hills Winery

Guests will move inside where a light antipasto course will be offered while the bridal party leaves to take photos.

Reception Party

Sat, 13th March, 7.00 PM - 11.00 PM

Brindabella Hills Winery

Guests will enjoy a canape style meal, with cutting of the cake, speeches and our first dance.

Brunch

Sun, 14th March, 11.00 am

Cafe Injoy, Gold Creek

Friends and family are welcome to join us for a late brunch the next morning. Please feel free to bring any children not present at the festivities the night before along.
